Centuri Analytics

As UI developer at Centuri Analytics, I have worked on a range of patient flow optimisation projects to develop innovative software solutions for different stages of the patient journey.

I have worked closely with senior consultants, nurses, finance specialists and process improvement managers to gather requirements, refine ideas and iterate quickly to produce software that enables more efficient department communication, enhances organisational capability, promotes patient safety, and increases compliance with department best practices.

My role at Centuri focuses on modern web application development with JavaScript and related technologies, but I also handle UX/UI design responsibilities, requirements gathering and QA for our patient flow management products, as well as delivering presentations to new and existing clients including senior consultants, business managers and C-Level hospital staff.

The first project I deployed with Centuri was LaminOS.ED, a Patient Flow Management System currently used by doctors, nurses and healthcare assistants to manage patients 24/7 in St. James's Hospital Emergency Department, one of the busiest Emergency Departments in Ireland.

In March 2017, the LaminOS.ED project was profiled by the Irish Times newspaper and recieved the cover feature of the health supplement.
